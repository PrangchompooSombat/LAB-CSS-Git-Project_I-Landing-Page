ABOUT US

Ditphong Ruangritthidet's family ancestors have been in Thailand since 1925, setting up a Chinese tea shop named Lim Meng Kee at Chalermburi Intersection on Yaowarat Road, importing tea products from China. But hot Chinese tea is not popular because Thailand has a hot climate. Therefore, red tea was imported to make milk tea. and Thai black tea with ice added. Known as iced tea until in 1945, the tea brand Cha Tra Mue began to grow tea itself in Chiang Rai. The factory is located in Bangkok. Ditphong later inherited the Cha Tra Mue business from his father. Established Siam FB Products Company Limited in 1994, which produced canned tea products. And later, the 3rd generation was Praownarin Ruangritthidet in 2018, releasing rose tea products and soft serve flavors such as Thai tea ice cream
